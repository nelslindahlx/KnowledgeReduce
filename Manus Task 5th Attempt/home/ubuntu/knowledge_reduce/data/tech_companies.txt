Apple Inc. was founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne in April 1976. The company is headquartered in Cupertino, California. Tim Cook is the current CEO of Apple. Apple released the iPhone in 2007, which revolutionized the smartphone industry.

Microsoft Corporation was founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen in April 1975. The company is headquartered in Redmond, Washington. Satya Nadella is the current CEO of Microsoft. Microsoft developed the Windows operating system, which is used by millions of people worldwide.

Amazon was founded by Jeff Bezos in July 1994. The company started as an online bookstore but has expanded to sell a wide variety of products. Amazon is headquartered in Seattle, Washington. Andy Jassy became the CEO of Amazon after Jeff Bezos stepped down in 2021.

Tesla, Inc. was founded by Martin Eberhard and Marc Tarpenning in July 2003. Elon Musk joined the company in 2004 and later became CEO. Tesla is headquartered in Austin, Texas. The company is known for its electric vehicles and clean energy products.

Google was founded by Larry Page and Sergey Brin in September 1998. The company is now a subsidiary of Alphabet Inc. and is headquartered in Mountain View, California. Sundar Pichai is the CEO of both Google and Alphabet. Google is known for its search engine and various internet services.
